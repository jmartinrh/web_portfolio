The Walking Dead Universe: Courtyard King (2025)

2025

Platform: Fortnite Creative / UEFN

Roles: Project Lead, Game & Level Designer

  • Adapted The Walking Dead IP into a 3-way king-of-the-hill style game with roguelite elements.
  • Led the project from concept to live-service launch.
  • Coordinated cross-discipline collaboration.
  • Designed and implemented progression, metagame, and enemy wave systems.
  • Built and greyboxed the core level layout, delivering immersive spaces faithful to the IP.

Core Challenges

Designing a dynamic enemy spawn system that could keep game areas challenging while prioritizing big enemy hordes in the capture points and making the interconnected level readable and navegable for all teams as capture points changed.

Boss Lab Project (2024)

2024

Platform: PC (UE 5.5)

Roles: Project Lead, Game Designer

  • Designed a boss-rush roguelike exploring player agency on enemy difficulty.
  • Designed modular skill and ability systems for both player and boss characters.
  • Designed a progression system that tied player empowerment to enemy difficulty.
  • Coordinated cross-discipline collaboration.
  • Conducted playtesting and player surveying to refine controls and balance.

Core Challenges

Designing modular character skills and abilities for enemies that could be mixed and matched during play and a counterintuitive approach to agency where the focus was not the player's power fantasy but their ability to control the challenge level of encounters.
